National Bird Day

The world is filled with a plethora of different species of birds. From cardinals to doves, from paraleets to parrots. Different people have different relationships with various species of birds. As such, one could easily imagine that there could actually be numerous National Bird Days in the world, serving different purposes and perharps carying by regions.

History of National Bird Day

The goal of the day is to educate folks on the destructive tendencies of the bird trade, the cruelty of bird breeding mills, and ideas for helping and improving the lives of birds that are already living in captivity.

How to Celebrate National Bird Day

Looking for ideas on how to celebrate National Bird Day? Well this list is a great way to begin;

  • Learn More About Birds
  • Visit Unique Birds in Their Natural Habitats
  • Help with Bird Conservation
  • Visit the National Bird Day Website
  • Get Educated on the Risks of Bird Extinction
